Project Overview

2024-2025

Project Timeline

2024 Activities

Activity 1: Kick-off Project Workshop
May 2-4, 2024

Strengthening the role of youth in the process of building peace and maintaining security. A shared responsibility between government institutions, youth organizations, and youth representatives.

Activity 2: Five-Day Training of Trainers (ToT)
June 9-13, 2024

Al-Iraqiya News Channel's coverage of training on Security Council Resolution 2250.

Activity 3: Training on UNSCR 2250 in 19 Governorates
August 13 - September 22, 2024

Comprehensive training program across all Iraqi governorates on UN Security Council Resolution 2250.

Activity 4: Three-Day Capacity-Building Training on Advocacy Strategies
July 6-8, 2024

Extensive media coverage across multiple channels including Al-Iraqiya, Zagros Arabic, Speda, Payam TV, and more.

Activity 5: Small Grants for Initiatives on UNSCR 2250
September 10 - December 17, 2024

Supporting youth-led initiatives implementing UN Security Council Resolution 2250.

Activity 6: Two-Day Roundtable Discussion
December 23-24, 2024

Towards a national plan for youth peace and security in Iraq.

2025 Activities

Activity 7: Small Grants All Over Iraq
March 2025

Implementation across 19 governorates including Erbil, Sulaimanya, Halabja, Baghdad, Misan, Mosul, Babil, Diwanya, Duhok, Waset, Najaf, Muthana, Kirkuk, Thiqar, Diyala, Basra, Salahaddin, Anbar, and Karbala.

Activity 8: Conducting Round Table
July 15, 2025

Strategic discussions on implementation and future directions.

Activity 9: Advocacy Campaign
January - May 2025

Comprehensive advocacy campaign including events in Baghdad and Sulaimanya, plus extensive social media outreach.

Activity 10: Final Conference
June 25, 2025

Project culmination event with comprehensive media coverage and stakeholder participation.
